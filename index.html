Unique gameplay, active community, fair rules, and zero pay-to-win.

Nexx SMP isn't just another server—it’s a community-driven experience where teamwork, creativity, and fair play thrive. We offer clan wars, base building, custom roles, and unique features—all without forcing purchases. Our staff are active, helpful, and the community is welcoming. Whether you're a warrior, builder, or strategist, this is the place for you.

Team up, build farms, fight players, roleplay, create machines and mansions, earn roles and perks, support the server, and collect keys!

There's a ton to do! You can find teammates and form clans, build huge farms or redstone machines, go PvP and battle other players, or roleplay your own story. Build anything from cozy homes to massive mansions. You can earn in-game roles, unlock perks, support the server, and get special crate keys to boost your progress!

Managing server costs while keeping it free and fun for everyone.

One big challenge has been handling the server's hosting and maintenance costs. But we don’t force anyone to pay—we believe in giving players the best free experience possible. Donations and support are appreciated, but never required. Everything we do is to keep the server enjoyable, fair, and accessible for all players.
